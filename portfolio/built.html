Zone Central — Corporate Intranet
Google Sites Still Active

Built the entire corporate intranet for Zone Entertainment in Google Sites. It serves as the single source of truth for all staff across 12 brands and 14 locations — a unified launchpad for every platform the company runs, organized by role and function. I built it from a blank site into the operational core the company still uses.

What's inside
Home — Quick-launch for Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Asana, HubSpot, Hire; search bar; Staff Dashboards + Ask AI buttons; brand grid with back-end links per location
Apps Hub — 30+ platform launcher: Google Workspace, Toast POS, Asana, HubSpot, Amazon Business, Canva, Adobe, Eventbrite, Curator, Scorecard App, all social platforms, all ad platforms (Meta Business Suite, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, SlickText, Amazon Ads, Roku, Spotify Ads, Snapchat Ads, Constant Contact), Cloudways, Cloudflare, AppSheet, Google Groups, Google Sites
Marketing Hub — Brand guidelines (colors, fonts, themes per brand), logo library organized by brand, Contact Exports
Operations Hub — Arcade game manuals library (including Corbin's Booklet — 22-page maintenance guide), kitchen/restaurant ordering, Eventbrite/EGG/Toast links
Web/Dev Hub — Google Admin, Cloud Console, Postmaster, Cloudflare, WPMU DEV, Zapier, staff support documentation
Corp Staff Matrix — Role-based "who does what" directory with schedule, contact method, and responsibilities per person
sites.google.com/zone-entertainment.com/central
